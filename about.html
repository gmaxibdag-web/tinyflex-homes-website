Our Story

Founded in 2024, TinyFlex Homes began with a simple vision: to make sustainable, affordable housing accessible to all Australians.

We saw the challenges facing Australian families and businesses - rising housing costs, limited space, and environmental concerns. Traditional construction was slow, expensive, and often wasteful.

Our solution was innovative yet practical: expandable container homes that combine the durability of shipping containers with smart, space-efficient design. Homes that could be transported easily, installed quickly, and expanded as needed.

Today, we're proud to be Australia's leading provider of expandable container homes, helping families, businesses, and communities create sustainable living and working spaces.
