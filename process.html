Our 6-Step Process

From initial consultation to move-in ready in 13 weeks

How It Works

A streamlined process designed for simplicity and speed

Step 1: Initial Consultation

Duration: 1-2 days

We start with a comprehensive consultation to understand your needs, site requirements, budget, and timeline. Our experts will:

  • Discuss your vision and requirements
  • Assess your site conditions and access
  • Explain expandable home options and benefits
  • Provide preliminary cost estimates

Step 2: Custom Design

Duration: 1-2 weeks

Our design team creates a customized plan for your expandable home:

  • Detailed floor plans and 3D renderings
  • Material and finish selections
  • Energy efficiency and sustainability features
  • Customization options (kitchen, bathroom, etc.)

Step 3: Final Quote & Approval

Duration: 2-3 days

Once you're happy with the design, we provide a detailed quote including:

  • Complete pricing breakdown
  • Manufacturing timeline (6-8 weeks)
  • Delivery and installation schedule
  • Payment plan options

Step 4: Factory Manufacturing

Duration: 8-10 weeks

Your expandable home is built in our controlled factory environment:

  • Precision engineering and construction
  • Quality control at every stage
  • Weather-independent manufacturing
  • Regular progress updates with photos

Step 5: Delivery

Duration: 1-2 weeks

We handle all logistics for Australia-wide delivery:

  • Professional transport coordination
  • Site access planning
  • Crane or forklift arrangements if needed
  • Delivery to your exact location

Step 6: Installation & Handover

Duration: 1-2 days

Our installation team completes the setup:

  • Professional installation and leveling
  • Connection to utilities (if required)
  • Final quality inspection
  • Comprehensive handover and orientation

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our process

How long does the entire process take?

From initial consultation to move-in ready: 13 weeks for most models. Commercial units may take 15-16 weeks.

Do I need council approval?

Most expandable homes under 100m² don't require council approval in many areas, but we'll guide you through local regulations and help with any required approvals.

What site preparation is needed?

You need a level, stable base (concrete slab, piers, or compacted gravel). We provide detailed site preparation guidelines.

Can I customize my expandable home?

Yes! We offer extensive customization options including layout, finishes, fixtures, and special features to match your needs.

What's included in the price?

Base price includes: manufactured home, delivery to site, basic installation, and 12-month warranty. Optional extras: site preparation, utility connections, custom finishes.
